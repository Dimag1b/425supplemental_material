The stock status tells other 7-11 goers what the stock level of their favorite item is currently. For example, if the 7-11 were out of coffee, you mark it a zero dude. If it's looking a little low, a three or a four might be better.

Basically, this is just a fancy way to help out your fellow CMU people and let everyone know if they're going to have a bad time getting taquitos, coffee, or donuts.
